ABOUT ME

designer/traveler/mountain biker

Hey. I'm Tavin. I wear many different hats at different moments, yet everything I do follows my true rhythm. I stand for design that enacts social change. I am a visionary. I am a problem solver. I am a big thinker. I am a dreamer.

WORK HARD

My dream is to work on the edge of innovation with engineers and product designers to create user experiences that our society never dreamed possible. In the present, I enjoy keeping up-to-date with every bit of creative software I can get my hands on. Adobe InDesign, Photoshop and Illustrator are my powerhouses, and I know Sketch as well. Through freelance work, I have learned HTML and CSS and am versing myself on Javascript from the ground up. Through my startup ventures, I have learned business, sales and marketing tactics, and pride myself in that I can speak the language of business people, engineers and designers.

PLAY HARD

I unwind by playing tennis, mountain biking in the hills of San Luis Obispo, and going backpacking around California. I also love taking photos wherever I go, deep conversations with friends, and drinking chocolate milkshakes. Additionally, I LOVE traveling. One aspiration of mine is to experience every country in Europe. I have been to 12 and plan to cross off a few more very soon.
