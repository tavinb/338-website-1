PULSE Health Education

Spring 2015
Developed with Drupal
pulse.calpoly.edu

Overhauled a site which was in disarray and not structured logically. I knew that the site needed more than a re-design. It needed to be re-envisioned from the ground up.

First, I wrote the information architecture on sticky notes, and re-arranged in most logical order, collaborating with my manager through every step.

While doing this, I considered many user profiles—What types of people will be visiting our site, and why are they coming to us?

I then inputted findings digitally and further refined the structure.
Last I Created mockups and iterated as necessary.

Next came the visual facelift of the site. I wanted it to be very image-heavy, which is both fun and usable. The following images are screenshots of the old site (left), versus the new site (right).

On the home page, I wanted to put a face to the name, for the business is very personable and friendly. This facilitates a welcoming atmosphere from the get-go.

I took advantage of making as many visual elements as possible.

Becuase I anticipate this site will not be kept-up regularly, I built in Google Calendar functionality that is always current.
